Problem statement

This unexpected and massive coexistence of cyclists on the streets and avenues generates more traffic accidents. As a consequence, cyclists, especially those who have been forced to turn to these means of mobility because of the pandemic, must be twice as attentive when riding on the street.

The lack of training in their reflexes, the noise pollution of traffic, the number of cars at rush hours, the lights and many other exogenous variables of the day to day, make the cyclist must be 300% more attentive and hope that luck is with him not to suffer an accident.

Approach to the solution

Our solution is the implementation of a guardian cyclist which is composed of 2 parts.

One was will composed with the oak d, a raspberry pi and a power bank to power them. This will be placed behind the bicycle seat pointing to the back traffic.

And the other main part will be two bracelets one for each user hand . Composed with a esp32 ( we use this board because his Bluetooth and wifi connectivity ) , a litio battery and a phone vibration motor. Here is the connection schema

Part 1 :: OAK-D + Raspberry Pi

The first part consists of connecting the oak-d with the rasperri Pi, we will use python as the programming language. With the python script in the raspberry pi we will make the car detection using spatial detection with openvino model to detect the cars behind us.

Part 2 :: Bracelet

The distance and position of the cars will be transformed to an intensity data and will be send via Bluetooth to one bracelet. If the car was 1 meter or less the intensity number will be 3, if the distance was more than 1 meter and less than 2, the intensity will be 2 and if the car is more than 2 meters the intensity will be 1.

To know which bracelet need to vibrate we transform the spatial x position of the car to pixel position over the screen making difference to left or right when the center of the detected car is more than 30 pixels of center of the screen.
